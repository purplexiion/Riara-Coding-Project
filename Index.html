Striving for Excellence

The Bright Future

At Riara, we provide all children with a safe and secure environment that encourages children to realise their full potential. As a centre of excellence, our standards are high and we strive to make learning an exciting and holistic experience.

Our Vision

"To be a centre of excellence in the provision of quality kindergarten, primary and high school education".

Our Mission

“To provide a holistic education that enables each learner to acquire knowledge, and to develop social and moral values, through a friendly learning environment where professionals help the student realize his or her highest potential”.

Our Schools and Insitutions

Riara International Schools

RIS offers quality education from Grades 1 -9 based on the British Curriculum. It is perfect for those seeking British Curriculum fit for their children. It is located in the heart of Riara Road opposite Makini Schools

Riara Road Primary Campus

Located in Riara Road, this campus offers CBC Curriculum from Kindergaten to Primary and is a top school in Nairobi during the national Examinations

Riara Springs Primary Campus

Located in Imara Daima, this campus also offers CBC Curriculum, producing quality education and top candidates in the country during National Examinations

Riara Springs Girls' High School

This Girls-only Highschool offers a comprehensive 8-4-4 Curriculum and is a perfect training ground for Girls, to mould them to be avenues of excellence.

Riara University

With the motto "Nurturing Innovators", the university provides holistic higher education to enable their students succeed in their passions and pursuits as they seek employment.
